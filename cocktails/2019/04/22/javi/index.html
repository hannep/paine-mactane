Expanse Cocktail #83
Javi

22 Apr 2019

Also known as “the bouillotte dealer”, Javi is a fixture on Ceres Station, always ready to throw down some cards and engage someone in a little casual gambling action. At the same time, he’s very knowledgeable about politics, current events, and the social effects those events have on the people of the Belt.

Ingredients:

  • 1½ ounces gold rum
  • 1½ ounces mango nectar
  • 1½ ounces grapefruit juice
  • ¾ ounce tequila (añejo)
  • ¾ ounce velvet falernum
  • ⅜ ounce lime juce
  • 1-2 dashes Angostura bitters

Instructions:

Rich Hibiscus Syrup: Bring 2 cups sugar and 1 cup water to a boil and remove from heat. Add 4 ounces dried hibiscus flowers and steep for 20 minutes. Strain, pressing the liquid through the saturated petals. Store in an airtight container up to 6 months.

Cocktail: Whip ¼ cup chilled, full-fat coconut milk with 2 tsp rich hibiscus syrup. Pour cocktail ingredients into a mixing glass with ice and stir until cold. Strain into a chilled hurricane glass with small ice cubes and garnish with hibiscus-coconut whipped cream.
