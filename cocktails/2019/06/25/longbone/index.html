Expanse Cocktail #93
Longbone

25 Jun 2019

The only name we have for the man who gets up in Havelock and Miller’s faces in a Ceres bar is “Longbone” — normally an epithet for spindly Belters, attenuated from a lifetime in low-G. He interrupts them to tell Havelock about the spurs at the top of Miller’s spine. The conversation then goes rapidly downhill from there.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pint lager
  • ¾ ounce aquavit
  • ½ ounce Amaro Ciociaro

Instructions:

Pour aquavit and ciociaro into a shot glass and stir gently to mix. Pour lager into a pint glass. Drop the shot glass into the lager as a bomb drink.
